Who Am I?

Hello!

This is the story of a little kid who started painting with broken crayons on the old walls of his home.

My parents always encouraged me to draw, no matter how messy it became. They smiled at every little sketch I made until they realized I might leave no wall untouched. That's when they handed me my very first sketchbook, and there has been no turning back ever since.

What began as childhood curiosity slowly became a lifelong passion.

Painting has always been my safe space. It excites me, challenges me, overwhelms me, and somehow, in the middle of it all, brings me peace. Whenever words fail me, colours find a way to speak. Every painting carries a small piece of who I am.

Today, I'm Subhajeet—a B.Tech student who still gets excited whenever he sees bright colours around him. Engineering has become a part of my life, but art has always been where I feel most alive. No matter where life takes me, I know I'll always return to a blank canvas with the same excitement I had as a child.

My Vision

My goal isn't just to create paintings. I want to create work that makes people pause, reflect, smile, or see the world from a different perspective. If even one person connects with my work, I believe it has served its purpose.

I want my art to inspire conversations, spread kindness, and remind people that beauty can often be found in the simplest moments.

My Journey

During my school years, I completed my Pre-Final Year in Painting, a journey that strengthened both my artistic skills and my discipline.

Since then, every painting has been a lesson—each mistake helping me grow and each finished piece reminding me why I fell in love with art.

I hope to use what I've learned to create meaningful work and connect with others who share the same love for creativity.

A Message To You

If you're reading this, thank you for being here.

Whether you're an artist, someone who loves art, or simply a curious visitor, I'm grateful our paths crossed today.

I hope these paintings brought you a little peace, a little joy, or perhaps reminded you of a memory you thought you'd forgotten.

Thank you for walking through my gallery.

Every painting here carries a small piece of my journey.

Perhaps one of them will stay with you long after you've left.

Until We Meet Again...
